Krishna a central figure in Hinduism,is revered as the eighth avatar of Lord Vishnu. Born in Mathura, he grew up in Gokul and later in Vrindavan. for his divine playfulness,enchanting flute melodies, and captivating personality, is a symbol of love, compassion, and wisdom.



Krishna is a beloved and iconic figure in Hindu mythology and folklore.Depicted as the mischievous and adorable child form of Lord Krishna,he is often portrayed with blue skin, a peacock feather in his hair,and a flute in his hands. The stories of Little Krishna are drawn from the ancient Indian scriptures, particularly the Bhagavata Purana and the Mahabharata.


A Krishna idol, often crafted from various materials like clay,marble, or metal, symbolizes the beloved Hindu deity Lord Krishna. With a divine and enchanting presence, these idols capture the essence of Krishna's teachings from the sacred Hindu scriptures, particularly the Bhagavad Gita.
